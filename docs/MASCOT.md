# 🧙‍♂️ Mentor Graph Mascot: The Sage

## 1. Concept Overview
The Mascot of Mentor Graph is **The Sage**, a digital embodiment of wisdom, clarity, and structured thinking. As users navigate the chaos of their intellectual influences, The Sage acts as their guide—helping them distill noise into structured knowledge.

## 2. Role in the App
The Sage is not just a visual decoration; it is an active guide in the user's journey to building their "Thinking OS".
- **Onboarding:** Welcomes the user and explains how to extract their mentor network.
- **Loading States:** Meditates or weaves data streams while the LLM processes text.
- **Insights Delivery:** Presents the "Insight & Optimisation" feedback. E.g., *"Ah, I see your mind leans heavily toward Engineering. Have you considered seeking wisdom in the realm of Wealth?"*
- **Empty States:** Encourages the user to input their first conversation.

## 3. Personality Traits
- **Wise & Patient:** Speaks with calm authority, never rushing the user.
- **Analytical & Precise:** Combines mystical wisdom with data-driven logic.
- **Encouraging:** Celebrates when the user balances their mentor graph or discovers a new domain.
- **Slightly Cryptic (but helpful):** Sometimes phrases insights like ancient proverbs before breaking them down into actionable steps.

## 4. Visual Identity
- **The Cloak:** Deep, cosmic blues and obsidian blacks (`#020617`, `#0f172a`), representing the depth of thought.
- **The Eyes:** Glowing cyan/neon blue (`#38bdf8`), piercing through the darkness, representing clarity and AI-driven insight.
- **The Beard:** Geometric, crystalline, or subtly polygonal, showing the intersection of ancient wisdom and modern technology.
- **The Staff (The Root Node):** Holds a staff topped with a glowing graph node, with smaller nodes orbiting it—representing the Mentor Graph itself.
- **Aura:** Surrounded by subtle, floating data particles and glowing constellations of connected nodes.

## 5. Voice & Tone Examples
- *Success:* "Your mentor graph expands. The foundations of your Thinking OS grow stronger."
- *Warning/Insight:* "A heavy reliance on one domain creates blind spots. Seek balance."
- *Loading:* "Sifting through the noise... distilling your influences."

## 10. Implementation Notes
- Use SVG for sharp, scalable rendering across the app.
- Apply CSS animations (e.g., pulsing glow on the staff, floating data particles, gentle hovering of the Sage) to bring it to life.
- Colour palette ties directly to app design tokens: deep indigo/charcoal base, blue→purple→pink gradient accents.
- Keep the design clean enough to fit a modern SaaS UI, but detailed enough to have a "wow" factor.
- Must be legible at 16×16 (browser tab) and impressive at 512×512 (hero).
